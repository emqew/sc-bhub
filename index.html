Foundational Brand Elements
Brand Guidelines
2025 v1.0
The Spectro Cloud visual brand expresses our mission of simplicity, control, and elegance. Follow these guidelines to ensure consistency across every touchpoint.
Logo
The Strata
Our symbol is inspired by origami — the folding motif combines simplicity with the form of an 'S'. The three parts represent the layers of our Cluster Profile stack. Always use approved files. Minimum width: 75px horizontal, 36px vertical.
Primary Palette
Colors
Deep, rich tones expressing warmth and calm. Tranquil Teal is the foremost color, balanced by Tea Green, Gold Leaf, Sunset Orange, and Soft Lilac.
Typography
Plus Jakarta Sans
A geometric typeface selected for smooth design. Free via Google Fonts. Fallback: Trebuchet.
HeadlineSimple mastery
SubheadTurn chaos into control
BodyEffortless Kubernetes management at any scale.
Brand Expression
The Fold
Our core expression is origami — the folding motif represents simplicity, flexibility, and hidden complexity. Use folded ribbons (one per layout), color blocking at 38.5°, and crease patterns as subtle background texture. Never place heavy patterns behind text.
Messaging
Short Boilerplate
"Turn the chaos of Kubernetes into effortless control, whatever the shape of your business."

Focus on simplicity, choice, flexibility, control. Reference pain points and business impact.
Brand Governance
Questions?
For simple asset use, work independently. For complex creative work — stand designs, co-branded assets, custom illustrations — share drafts before publishing.
